SPECIFICATIONS FOR OPULENCIA - 2/3/4 BHK

Flooring

Marble /granite / vitrified tiles in Drawing-Dining room, laminated wooden flooring in master bedroom, vitrified tiles in others bedrooms, anti skid tiles for balcony and granite/kota stone for lift lobby.

Toilets

Walls tiled up to the required height with ceramic and glazed tiles, flooring done with anti-skid ceramic tiles, wash basin and wall mounted , European WC, branded chrome plated brass fittings. Geyser facility in toilets.

Kitchen

Modular kitchen, polished granite workstations, glazed tiles up to 2ft. height above the counter, chrome plated brass fittings and provision for hot and cold water supply with Chimney & RO.

Windows

Ultra unique designed GPSP/powder coated aluminium glazed windows with toughened glass and fixed panels and normal glass on wire mesh panels.

Doors

Seasoned wood frame with Solid wood main Door, flush doors for Bedrooms and powder coated moulded GPSP/aluminium glazed frames with glass opening in balcony. Provision for wire mesh doors.

Hardware

Hinges and all hardware of good quality, all doors except those in toilets to be provided with a mortise locks.

Internal Finishes

POP cornices, Acrylic emulsion in all rooms. Dry distemper in utility / servant room.

Electrical works

Copper wiring, concealed conduits, protective MCB, modular switches and provision for sufficient power and light points in all rooms. TV and telephone sockets in drawing, dining and bedrooms. Provision for Ceiling fan in drawing/dining and bedroom area and exhaust fans in toilets, tube lights in drawing dinning and bedrooms, CFL in toilets.

Structure

The complex is erected by earthquake resistant RCC framed structure to ensure precise safety against any natural calamity.
